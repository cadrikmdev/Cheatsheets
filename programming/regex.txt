^   - line starts with
$   - line ends with
.   - 1 any character
?   - 1 or none character
*   - 0 or more 
+   - 1 or more
{n} - n occurences
{n,} - at least n occurences
{n,m} - between n and m occurences
[0-9]   - any 1 from list
[^abc]   - any despite of a, b, c
[[:digit:]]	numbers
[[:alpha:]]	characters
[[:alnum:]]	characters + numbers
[[:space:]]	spaces, tabs
[[:lower:]]	lowercase characters
[[:upper:]]	uppercase characters
